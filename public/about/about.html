About #SquadGoals:

Squad Goals is an app that uses your FitBit account to track the progress of you and your colleagues in a competitive work-friendly way. #SquadGoals has a unique and one-of-a-kind design where you are able to see self-improvements while cheering for your group.


Create your own challenges that allow you to compete department against department, company versus company, city-wide or even globally. Dominate the board and collect unique badges. Meet your fitness goals as a department or as a whole company, the decision is up to you. Gather up your squad and achieve your goals! #SquadGoals!
