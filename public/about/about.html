About #SquadGoals:

Squad Goals was designed to track the progress of you and your co-workers in a competitive work friendly way. #SquadGoal's has a unique and one of a kind design, where you are able to track each others progress while cheering for your Company.


With #SquadGoals, you and co-workers can compete locally and or city wide with many different challenges. Dominate the board and collect badges. Target goals as a department or as whole company, the decision is up to you. Gather up your squad and achieve some goals! #SquadGoals!!
