About Squad Goals:

Squad Goals is an app that uses your Fitbit account to track the progress of you and your colleagues in a competitive, work-friendly way. See self-improvements while cheering for your group. Aspire to earn team MVP, dominate the challenge board, and collect unique badges.


Companies: create your own fitness challenges that allow you to encourage employees to develop stronger team dynamics while also promoting healthy competition - within the company, city-wide, or even globally. Gather up your squad and achieve those fitness goals! #SquadGoalsapp
